Ordering Process

This is so called the Purchase to Pay process.

Here you can find the most important rules in the process:

  • One Purchase order (called PO) per delivery placed by Flora. Purchase order includes
    1. Purchase order number (in short called PO).
    2. Our contract number (starts with 460...)
    3. Supplier contract number.
    4. Price for the oil per tonne.
    5. Quantity in tonnes needed.
    6. Loading date.
    7. Place of delivery.
    8. Bill to address.
    9. Incoterm.
    10. Example of the PO (Purchase order) Click
  • Supplier has to confirm the receipt of the PO (Purchase order) as soon as possible in a preferable way.
  • Supplier has to inform Upfield if any details are missing or incorrect on the Purchase order, like price for example (to avoid invoicing issues).

Other Rules

  • Any amendments done to contracts due to e.g. price or delivery period changes, need to be confirmed by both parties before any amendments to the contract are made.
  • Supplier to submit contract balance overview every Monday to make sure contract positions are aligned. The overview shall include:
    1. Contract number
    2. Validity period
    3. Material number and description
    4. Contracted volume
    5. Open balance
    6. Unit price and currency
    7. Upfield entity/plant description to bill to
    8. Incoterms
    9. Loading location
  • Supplier to provide contact details of Account Manager, Sales Representative, Logistics, Customer Service, Quality Department (email & phone number with the function).
